Measure 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken and add it to a small microwave-safe dish
Measure 2 cups frozen corn.
Thaw the frozen corn and add it to the microwave-safe dish
Measure 1/4 cup pico de gallo and add it to the microwave-safe dish
Cover and cook on high for 1-2 minutes or until heated through.
Spoon chicken mixture into taco shells.
Measure 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
Measure 1 cup coleslaw mix
Take 3 jalapeno peppers and slice them
Measure 1/2 cup chipotle ranch salad dressing
Top the tacos with all the ingredients taken above
