Put the butter and chocolate into a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on high for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds to melt the chocolate.
Break 2 eggs in a bowl and throw away the shells.
Take 1 cup of sugar and add it to the bowl.
Whisk together the eggs and sugar.
In another bowl, sift 1/2 cup all-purpose flour on top of 1/2 teaspoon baking powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Add the egg and chocolate mixtures to the bowl.
Measure 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, add it to the bowl and stir the ingredients.
Spray a microwave-safe glass pan with cooking spray.
Pour the batter into the pan by spreading it out evenly.
Measure 1/2 cup of chocolate chunks and scatter them on top.
Cook in the microwave for 5 minutes.
Remove from the oven and rest for 3 minutes before cutting and eating.
