Peel 2 medium potatoes.
Slice 2 peeled medium potatoes.
Slice one medium onion.
Coat a 9-in. microwave-safe plate with cooking spray
Arrange potato and onion slices on plate
Sprinkle potato and onion with seasonings(garlic salt, salt, pepper).
Cover the plate and microwave on high for 9 minutes or until potatoes are tender
Add cheese on the top in the last 30 seconds of cooking.
