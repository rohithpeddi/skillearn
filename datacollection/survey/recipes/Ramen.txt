Remove the noodles from the package. (Break Noodles / Keep them as a block).
Chop celery.
Finely Chop carrot.
Chop garlic.
Finely chop onions.
Take peas out of the bag.
Put all the Vegetables in a microwave-safe bowl.
Add the noodles to the bowl and cover them with water. (1 to 2 cups of water - Depends on broth).
Cover with a lid (or paper towel) to prevent splattering.
Microwave the ramen for between three and five minutes.
Measure basil and cilantro and add them to the bowl.
Cut a boiled egg into pieces and add it to the bowl.
Let the noodles sit for about three minutes after the microwave stops.
Mix in the flavour packet. Stir noodles with a spoon or fork until the flavouring dissolves.
