Cut two medium carrots into short, thin strips (Julienne)
Slice 1 small onion
Cut 1 small green pepper into short, thin strips (Julienne)
Mince 1 garlic clove
Place meatballs in a 3-qt. Microwave-safe dish.
Top with carrots, onion, pepper and garlic.
In a small bowl, mix sweet-and-sour sauce and soy sauce; pour over meatballs.
Microwave, covered, on high for 6-8 minutes or until vegetables are tender and meatballs are heated through, stirring twice.
Serve with rice.
