Recipe: Cucumber Raita
-----------------------------------------------------
Description: 
In a mixing bowl, whisk 1 cup of chilled curd until smooth.
Use fresh homemade or packaged curd.
Avoid using sour-tasting curd.
Rinse, peel and chop or grate 1 medium-sized cucumber (You need 1 cup of chopped or grated cucumber).
Add the chopped or grated cucumber to the whisked curd.
Add 1 teaspoon of cumin powder, 1/2 teaspoon of chaat masala powder, 1/4 teaspoon of red chilli powder and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
Lastly, add 1 tablespoon of chopped cilantro leaves
Combine all the ingredients.
