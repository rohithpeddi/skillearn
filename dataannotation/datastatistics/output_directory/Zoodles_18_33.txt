Recipe: Zoodles
-----------------------------------------------------
Description: 
Spiralize 1 medium zucchini into thin noodles using a spiralizer.
Heat a large pan on medium heat.
Melt 1 tablespoons of softened butter.
Peel 1 garlic cloves.
Add 1 large minced garlic cloves to the pan.
Cook garlic until fragrant (about 1 minutes).
Be careful not to burn garlic.
Add the zucchini noodles and 1/6 cup grated parmesan cheese and season with salt & pepper to taste.
Cook for 1 minutes or until the zoodles are done.
Remove from heat and serve immediately.
Top with more parmesan if desired.
