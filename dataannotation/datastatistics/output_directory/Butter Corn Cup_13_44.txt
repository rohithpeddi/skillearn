Recipe: Butter Corn Cup
-----------------------------------------------------
Description: 
Measure 2 cups of frozen corn.
Thaw the frozen corn by putting it in a sieve and running it under cold water.
Add the corn into a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave the corn for 2 minutes.
Add 1 teaspoon of softened butter and 1 teaspoon of pepper powder to the bowl and then stir.
Microwave the corn for 3 more minutes.
Add 1 teaspoon salt to the bowl.
Extract lime juice from 1/3 lime and add it to the bowl.
Mix the contents of the bowl well.
