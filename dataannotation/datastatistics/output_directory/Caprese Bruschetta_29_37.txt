Recipe: Caprese Bruschetta
-----------------------------------------------------
Description: 
Slice 2 1/2 inch thick rounds from a baguette (slice slanted)
Brush 2 slices of baguette with olive oil on both sides.
Toast both sides of the slices on the pan for 2 to 3 minutes until lightly charred and crispy.
Cut 1/4 cup of cherry tomatoes into halves.
In a bowl, add the cut cherry tomatoes, 1/8 cup shredded mozzarella, 1/16 cup basil, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper.
Combine the contents of the bowl.
Spoon the mixture onto the bread.
