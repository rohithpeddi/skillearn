Recipe: Spicy Tuna Avocado Wraps
-----------------------------------------------------
Description: 
Take 1 ripe avocado and cut it into thin slices.
Chop 1 scallion.
Open a can of tuna and drain excess water.
Add 1 can drained tuna, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 1 tsp Sriracha sauce, and chopped scallion in a bowl.
Mix the contents of the bowl.
Season with 1/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.
Lay out 2 large lettuce leaves, place avocado slices on each leaf, and top with the tuna mixture.
Roll up the lettuce wraps and secure wrap with a toothpick.
