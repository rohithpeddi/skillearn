Shape beef into 3-in. pattie.
Place on a microwave-safe plate lined with paper towels. Cover with another paper towel;
Microwave on high for 1 minute until meat is no longer pink
Thinly chop onions
Thinly chop  pickles
Thinly chop 1 tomato
Spread half the bread circle.
Layer with a cooked burger patty, 1 slice of cheese, onions, pickles, tomatoes and salad dressing.
Top with remaining bread circle; secure with a toothpick.
