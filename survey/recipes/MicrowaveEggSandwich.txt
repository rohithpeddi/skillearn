Coat 6-oz. ramekin or custard cup with cooking spray.
Place Canadian bacon on the bottom of a 6-oz. ramekin or custard cup.
Pour egg substitute on top.
Microwave the ramekin or custard cup uncovered on high for 30 seconds and stir.
Continue to Microwave for 15-30 more seconds or until egg is almost set.
Top with 1 tablespoon of salsa and sprinkle with cheese.
Microwave just until cheese melts, about 10 seconds.
Line bottom of English muffin with spinach.
Take out egg and Canadian bacon from the cup, place it over spinach and replace the top of english muffin.
