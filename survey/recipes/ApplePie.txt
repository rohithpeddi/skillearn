Place the crust into a microwave-safe pie dish.
Score (press) the bottom and sides of the crust with a fork.
Microwave on high for 5 minutes and let it rest while you make the fillings.
Take the apples and peel them.
Then slice the apples into pieces.
Add the apples to the microwave-safe bowl.
Measure flour (3 tablespoons), white sugar (1/4 cup), brown sugar (1/4 cup), cinnamon (1 teaspoon) and vanilla extract (1 teaspoon) and add to the bowl.
Extract the juice from 1/2 lemon and add it to the bowl.
Mix all the ingredients well.
Microwave on high for 2.5 minutes.
Stir the contents in the bowl.
Again, Microwave on high for 2.5 minutes.
Add the filling (from the microwave) on top of the crust.
Measure brown sugar (1 cup ), flour (1/2 cup ), and quick oats (1/2 cup) and put the ingredients mentioned above into a bowl.
Cut the cold butter (1/2 cup) and put it in the bowl.
Add the crumble top (from the bowl) to the pie (with crust and filling).
Microwave for 5 minutes and take the pie out.
